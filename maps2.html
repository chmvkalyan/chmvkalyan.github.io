Cases Per Country Over Time

The below map depicts the geographgic distribution of COVID-19 Cases across the World. The color graident changes from light yellow to dark orange ranging low to high "Cases (Per Million)".

While the slider faciliates the option to choose the time frame, the tooltip helps you with more details including - Population, Deaths (per Million), Tests (per Thousand), and Vaccinations (per Hundred).

The Countries in the Continents North America, South America and Europe have major hotspots because the Cases (per Million) is higher compared to the Countries in Asia. Hence they appear darker than the highly populated Countries India, and China.
