Population Vs. Cases

The following scatter plot represents the Population versus COVID-19 total cases reported worldwide. Population on X-axis, Total cases on Y-axis, Color indicates the Continent, and Size of the circle indicates the Count of deaths. Hover over the datapoints (circles) to see the additional details through the tool tip option. Annotation describes that the Top countries that are highly impacted reporting highest number of total cases and deaths in the world so far.
